Public Relations and Marketing

What you need to do for Marketing

Start marketing from Day 1. Use Social Media, funnel to a newsletter, forum, or Discord server. Have continued interaction with your community. Go to events, have people playtest your game, and gauge the reaction from your users. Build assets for marketing to give your best first impression. Create a PR Timeline and schedule out your marketing. Set goals for marketing. Try to write / pitch articles that will make your game or studio standout and get noticed. What is unique about the development team? If your plan is to sell copies, plan a marketing campaign. You have to spend money to make money. A minimum budget for traditional marketing is around $50,000 dollars.

Goals of a Landing Page

Show off your game. Use the best screenshots, videos, etc.

Have a Call-to-Action that incentivizes. Offer demo, soundtrack, updates, newsletter, digital assets, concept art, etc. in exchange for email.

You have to give value to get your core audience invested.

Importance of A/B Testing and Analytics

PR has quantifiable data such as clicks, views, and acquisition. What is bad about the marketing material? The Header? The Content? The Media? Use A/B Testing to improve your marketing pages. The goal is to use A/B Testing to reduce the cost of an acquisition and optimize your marketing strategy. Who is your target demographic? Your demographic might be different than you originally thought. Consider adjusting your marketing accordingly. Use analytic tools on social media and Google. They will help determine how effective your social media posts and adverts are.

How to get the most help from News Outlets

It takes time to build relationships with other people. Be precise with your PR approach. Build a relationship with journalists. Once that is established, then pitch your game to them. Easiest way to get News Outlets to help is to prove that your game has the potential for success. Show them that your game is launching and has a community.

How to best partner with Media Outlets

Make a Press Kit. A press kit provides media outlets have all the essential information and images to use in their articles or videos. Give creative freedom to the author or content creator. If you are being interviewed, be honest and say things with 100% certainty. Do not make assumptions about what will be in your game. Avoid using licensed music from the Music Industry in videos/trailers. This could result in a DMCA takedown on the video, a copyright strike on the YouTube Channel, or lawsuit.
