About

GameDevelop.io provides Game Developers of any skill level with the information and resources necessary to develop Video Games.

The Resource Database strives to be a complete and up to date collection of tools, assets, communities, and services commonly used by game developers.

The Guides provides critical knowledge so game developers can tackle both the production and business sides of game development.

History

GameDevelop.io was originally conceived as a Video Game Industry job aggregator, until several well made aggregators were discovered. Realizing there was a lack of a well maintained database for game development resoruces, GameDevelop.io pivoted to fill that need.

During the construction of the resource database, it became apparent that game developers needed guidance in other aspects of game development. Guides were written to help new and seasoned developers about games production, business, and marketing.

GameDevelop.io plans to continue growing the resource database, and providing helpful guides.
